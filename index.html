About Me

Hi, I'm Miky. I offer calm, one-to-one dog grooming sessions in a relaxed home environment. Every dog receives personal attention, gentle handling, and a tailored approach.

Whether your pet needs a full groom, a quick bath, or a careful nail trim, I make sure each visit is stress-free and comfortable.
